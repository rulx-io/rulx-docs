Diese Gruppe deckt infrastrukturseitige Runtime-APIs ab.

## Runner-Endpunkte

- `GET /v1/runners`
- `GET /v1/runners/{runnerId}`
- `POST /v1/runners/{runnerId}/drain`
- `POST /v1/runners/{runnerId}/undrain`

## Service-Endpunkt

- `GET /v1/services`
