title: 'Workspace settings and access'
description: 'The settings surface for workspace ownership and workspace-scoped controls'
---

Settings are where workspace-level control lives.

## What belongs here

The settings area is the right place for:

- workspace identity and ownership
- member and access management
- workspace-scoped operational settings
- package registry configuration where dependency workflows need it

## Why it matters

Rulx is intentionally workspace-scoped. That means many operational and governance choices belong in workspace settings instead of in global runtime configuration.
