---

The workspace overview is the operational landing page for a workspace.

It is designed to answer:

- What is happening right now?
- What needs attention?
- What should I do next?

## Overview

The overview surfaces summary and performance views for the current workspace.

Common signals include:

- recent failures
- queue and latency issues
- throughput and success trends
- controller availability

## Alerts

Alerts are curated operational issues rather than raw logs.

Use them to focus quickly on:

- controller unavailability
- new failed runs
- elevated latency or queue time
- other attention-worthy workspace signals
