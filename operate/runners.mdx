The infrastructure surfaces focus on the execution fleet rather than on a single workspace artifact.

## Runners

Runners are the execution role for leased work.

The controller exposes public runner registry endpoints and the UI gives you a runner-focused operational view.

Common tasks:

- list runners
- inspect a runner
- drain a runner
- undrain a runner

## Services and runtime health

The controller also exposes service-level runtime endpoints used by the operator-facing surfaces.
