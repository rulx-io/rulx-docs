The flow editor is where you shape the graph that will execute at runtime.

## What you edit here

The editor focuses on:

- nodes
- edges
- selection and inspection
- editor-mode changes to the active draft

## Related surfaces

In practice, you move between:

- **General** for identity and summary settings
- **Config** for callable interface and entrypoint details
- **Tests** for trigger and test-case related work
- **Runs** for debugging execution history
